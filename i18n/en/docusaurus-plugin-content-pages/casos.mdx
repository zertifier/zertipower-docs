# Cases

Explore how teams apply the project in different contexts. Our agriculture data space is already deployed for soil data
analysis on fields in the Azores Islands (Portugal). Here you will find cards per field/service with access to datasets,
algorithms, and granular access policies, all under data sovereignty with Compute‑to‑Data.
